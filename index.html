About

People Science in Practice

Rich Cuervo is an HR professional and researcher focused on the behavioral and organizational dimensions of work — how people lead, how cultures form, and why systems succeed or fail.

His work spans motivational theory, emotional intelligence, organizational effectiveness, and the human cost of broken workplace systems. He writes for practitioners who believe that evidence-based approaches to people can transform organizations from the inside out.

Rich holds an MBA and carries credentials including SHRM-SCP, SPHR, and HCM. He is available for speaking engagements, consulting partnerships, and collaborative research opportunities.
